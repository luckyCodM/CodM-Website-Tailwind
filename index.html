Sales Cloud

  • Salesforce Sales Cloud is perhaps the company’s most popular cloud. It automates the sales process, drives lead generation, and facilitates communication across multiple channels.

Service Cloud

  • Salesforce Service Cloud is a cloud-based platform for customer service management. It functions as a help desk and customer service platform and enables companies to provide personalized customer support.
OPEN NOW

Experience Cloud

  • Salesforce Experience Cloud, formerly known as Salesforce Community Cloud, is a platform that allows businesses to create branded and personalized online experiences for their customers, partners, and employees.
OPEN NOW

Financial Services Cloud

  • Salesforce Financial Services Cloud is a cloud-based solution that provides secure connectivity for financial service organizations with their customers, partners, and employees. It serves as a unified platform for managing customer data, applications, and processes in the financial sector.
OPEN NOW

Nonprofit Cloud

  • Salesforce Nonprofit Cloud is a cloud-based software as a service (SaaS) offering that equips nonprofits with a comprehensive set of tools to manage their organizations. It includes features for donor management, fundraising, program management, and constituent engagement.
OPEN NOW

Health Cloud

  • Empower hospitals and care givers with a unified view of users through Salesforce Healthcare Cloud. Personalise engagement with patients, care providers, partners and employees. Deliver differential treatment, wishful healthcare needs and special care remotely.

Manufacturing Cloud

  • Salesforce Manufacturing Cloud is a service aimed at helping manufacturers efficiently manage production processes and supply chains. It offers a complete view of the manufacturing lifecycle, from raw materials to finished products, enabling optimization of operations and improvement of overall performance.

Education Cloud

  • Salesforce Education Cloud is a cloud-based solution designed for educational institutions to effectively manage their data and operations. It offers a centralized platform for student data management, progress tracking, and stakeholder communication.
