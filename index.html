SALESFORCE CRM SERVICES

Sales Cloud

Salesforce Sales Cloud is perhaps the company’s most popular cloud. It automates the sales process, drives lead generation, and facilitates communication across multiple channels.

Service Cloud

Salesforce Service Cloud is a cloud-based platform for customer service management. It functions as a help desk and customer service platform and enables companies to provide personalized customer support.

Experience Cloud

Salesforce Experience Cloud, formerly known as Salesforce Community Cloud, is a platform that allows businesses to create branded and personalized online experiences for their customers, partners.

Manufacturing Cloud

Salesforce Manufacturing Cloud is a service aimed at helping manufacturers efficiently manage production processes and supply chains. It offers a complete view of the manufacturing lifecycle

Education Cloud

Salesforce Education Cloud is a cloud-based solution designed for educational institutions to effectively manage their data and operations. It offers a centralized platform for student data management.

Health Cloud

Empower hospitals and care givers with a unified view of users through Salesforce Healthcare Cloud. Personalise engagement with patients, care providers, partners and employees.

Nonprofit Cloud

Nonprofit Cloud is a cloud-based software as a service (SaaS) offering that equips nonprofits with a comprehensive set of tools to manage their organizations. It includes features for donor management, fundraising.

Financial Services Cloud

Salesforce Financial Services Cloud is a cloud-based solution that provides secure connectivity for financial service organizations with their customers, partners, and employees. It serves as a unified platform for managing.
